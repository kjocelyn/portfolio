Design Projects

The Nishikigoi logo has two koi fish swimming together to represent a network. Red and black colors were used because they are known to be koi fish colors. These two colors also demonstrate accented harmony. Logo scalability for different sizes are also shown.

Icons were created based on the Nishikigoi logo. The color red was used to mirror the color theme of the original logo.

Since the subject of the app is "koi," I decided to mirror the traditional aspect of it by making it look as clean and simple as possible. Since the app is meant to build a koi keeping network, it is important to use a structure that is easy for users to follow and a typeface that is easy to read. Accented harmony is shown throughout the wireframe to emphasize the subject, including UI elements.

Software: Illustrator

The purpose of this project is to raise awareness about koi fish and provide a network for those who appreciate and want to learn more about the different species.
